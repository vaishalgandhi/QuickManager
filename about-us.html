About Us

Quick Manager is management tool which manages the tasks which are either HRâ€™s responsibility or Project Manager.It is designed in such a way that it can provide functionality of different software at one place.Motive behind this product is to get everything at one place.

Our main target is small and medium size Company who works in IT industry. This product is best suitable for IT industry.Although it can also serve to other companies as well. This product is very handy for start-up companies or medium size company who wants to go digitalization.

Payroll - Our product will let HR modify auto generated payroll so that if there is any special allowance or overtime can directly done through the system. Employees can directly download their payslip from their account.

Employee management - Our product provide ability to add or update any employee at any time in the system. Further more they can create new department if required. They can manage leave request of employee.

Project management - Using Quick Manager, PMs can manage their projects by adding them, keep an eye on timeline, completion percentage, assigning team members and assigning task to team members.

Social Networking - HR can view followers and like statistics in the system only as well as they can directly post something on facebook, twitter, LinkedIn without login to their individual account.

Chat - There are lot of chat applications that are available on the internet but including chat application in this makes the data on our own server, it can use internally can sent text message, pictures, video, files as well as video calling is available. It is eliminating use of other app.

Support Ticket - This system will basically work as E-mail system so that you can raise ticket as E-mail and then your PM allow the ticket and support guy will help you out. This feature is built in a way that you can use this as ordinary email client as well.

You can manage Payroll, Employee, Projects can use social networks accounts, can discuss internally and have an email client environment all at one place which is motive of this product.
